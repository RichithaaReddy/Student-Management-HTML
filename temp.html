ABOUT


The Cherabuddi Education Society was registered in January 1999 as an NRI-promoted society. The Society aims at creating a state-of-the-art engineering institution in association with leading NRI technology professionals and well-known academicians of the twin states of Telangana & Andhra Pradesh. The Society aims to harness technical excellence with a commitment to the ethos of 'useful learning'. Read the Full Story

Vision of the Institute

To excel in providing quality education at under graduate and graduate levels. To encourage research and innovation. To provide infrastructure and facilities to meet the latest technological needs. To establish Centres of Excellence through active interaction with industry. To nurture students towards holistic development with human values and ethics.
